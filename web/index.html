Duplicate the template into your Notion

Your hub lives in your own Notion workspace. Open the dotfound template, then click the duplicate icon  at the top-right of the page to copy it in. All four databases and their links come across together. Then come back here to tailor it.

Fields

The properties on a record: the columns you'd see in a Notion table. Short, structured, filterable values you set directly.

e.g. a client's Status, Sector, Hourly Rate.

Body

The written page inside a record. Claude composes these sections from your linked work and connected sources. You set the headings and their meaning, it fills them in.

e.g. a project's Context, Scope, Deliverables.
